Welcome to my page!

My name is Arri and this is my page where you can learn a bit about me!

I'm currently a student in 3A of the Computer Science & Business Double Degree program, pursuing a Honours Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of Waterloo and a Honours Bachelor of Business Administration at Wilfred Laurier University.



I am currently seeking a co-op/internship position for the Winter 2019 term (January-April). I am primarily interested in software engineering and product management, but I am open to everything!

I love creating things, from music to writing to design & code. I am currently heading the Translation team at the Foundation for Student Science and Technology (fsst.ca), a non-profit nationally published youth science journal. Beyond that, I have lots of volunteering experience as I love to involve myself with events and the school community, meet cool people and to learn! I have worked with various organizations and events, including Science Expo, Brampton Multicultural Youth Council, Campus Vibez, Discover Your Personal Brand, Rogers Cup, YMCA, The ScotiaBank Marathon, Toronto Zoo, and Music Makes Us.

I also attending hackathons! I see them as a great learning resource, as you get to focus on one project and have lots of people around you for support with whatever you build. Also great for networking and meeting cool people :)

Hackathons: Hack the North, UofT Hacks, Start Hacks, Starter Hacks, OPS Code Day, MHacks X, Y Hacks, RU Hacks, ETHWaterloo, PennApps and more...

Otherwise, I'm into many activities, from tennis, table tennis, badminton, jazz (any tenor sax/clarinet players?), aca pella, beatbox, to gaming (Kongregate, Smite, League of Legends) and deal hunting.

So feel free to get in touch!

Projects

Disaster Simulator

Game built in Unity using C# to promote disaster preparedness and resiliency! First use of Unity!

ReadR

Android app that allows you to keep track of your favourite reads! Tried implementing SnapKit. Made with Android Studio.

Insight.ai

Webapp that uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) algorithms to predict mood and mindstate of given text (eg.diary entries), and alerts on at-risk mindstates and helps schedule events in Google Drive. React/Material-UI/AWS/Flask/Tensorflow. (Video Demo)

Recipic

Mobile app that allows users to take pictures of leftover food, and using the Clarafai API, identifies the food and recommends recipes within the app. Built on the Ionic platform, with HTML/CSS/JS.

Unharassthe.net

Website that advocates for a safer net, and teaches users to turn down cyberbullying. Includes an interactive quiz that uses the Leapmotion controller. Uses HTML/CSS/JS.

Turner Fenton Music Database

Microsoft Access Relational Database that keeps track of instruments, sign-outs, statuses and repairs. Uses SQL to run queries and generate reports on backend. Designed with functional frontend menus.

arriye.me

My own site, that you see right now! Exploring web design concepts, hosting, git version control, and playing around with CSS formatting. Hosted on Github pages, uses HTML/CSS/JS.

CryptoBot

Chatbot that allows you to buy/sell Cryptocurrencies like bitcoin in realtime using the Coinbase service(API/authentication). Also lets you visualize historical data with React. Node.js, React, Azure, HTML/CSS/JS.
